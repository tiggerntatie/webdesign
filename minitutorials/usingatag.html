How to use <a> tags

The <a> is how you give your reader clickable links to other web pages. An example of typical use is:

visit the <a href="www.google.com">Google search engine</a> for a great search experience

The URL of the link is inserted in the <a> tag itself as an attribute/value pair. The attribute is "href" and the value is the URL of the link. The text that your reader will see is inserted between the <a> and </a>.
